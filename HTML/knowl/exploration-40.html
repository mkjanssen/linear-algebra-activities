Exploration 6.1.1. (Strang 5.2.2).
Compute determinants of the following matrices using the big formula. Are the columns of these matrices linearly independent?
\begin{equation*} A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}, B = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \end{pmatrix}, C = \begin{pmatrix} A & 0 \\ 0 & A \end{pmatrix}, D = \begin{pmatrix} A & 0 \\ 0 & B \end{pmatrix}. \end{equation*}
