Denote the owl and wood rat populations at time \(k\) by the vector \(x_k = \begin{bmatrix} O_k \\ R_k \end{bmatrix}\text{,}\) where \(k\) is the time in months, \(O_k\) the number of wols in the region studied, and \(R_k\) the numer of rats (measured in thousands). Suppose

\begin{align*} O_{k+1} &= (0.5) O_k + (0.4)R_k\\ R_{k+1} &= (-p) O_k + (1.1)R_k, \end{align*}

where \(p\) is a positive parameter to be specified.
